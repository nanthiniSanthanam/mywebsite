About AI Chat

This chat demonstrates integration with an LLM (Large Language Model) API. In a real application, your messages would be sent to a backend server, which would then forward them to an AI service like OpenAI's GPT or Anthropic's Claude.

For this demo, responses are simulated but show how you would implement the frontend portion of an AI chatbot.

Try asking:

  • "Hello, how are you today?"
  • "What's the weather like?"
  • "Can you help me with programming?"
  • "What time is it right now?"
  • "Tell me a fun fact about AI"
